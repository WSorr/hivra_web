Story Architecture Intent
Core frame

What the public site is trying to make legible.

The goal is not just to present Hivra as a name. It is to make the system readable: what a capsule is, how starters differ, and why relation architecture matters.

Capsules
  • Bounded network units. Capsules act as containers for starter logic, role, and growth state.
  • Different maturity levels. Genesis and Proto capsules signal whether a node is fully seeded or still emerging.
  • Readable at a glance. The visualization layer reduces explanation overhead by exposing state visually.
Relationships
  • Not just connections. Relationships in Hivra are structured pathways with direction, inheritance, and meaning.
  • Trust can be selective. Starters and capsule states allow different relation surfaces to emerge without flattening the system.
  • Coordination becomes composable. The network is easier to reason about when relation logic is explicit.
Value flow
  • Designed instead of assumed. Exchange is framed as intentional routing through a relationship architecture.
  • Surface before scale. The site focuses on clarity now so the model can grow without losing coherence later.
  • Public artifact first. The current build establishes language, visual grammar, and navigational structure.
Starter model

Five signals that define the current starter vocabulary.

These are the starter types visualized on the network page. Each one implies a different quality of motion, initiation, or continuity inside a capsule.

J

Juice

Energy, fuel, and immediate activation potential.

S

Spark

Ignition, volatility, and the first visible pulse of action.

Sd

Seed

Continuity, planting, and future-bearing relation logic.

P

Pulse

Rhythm, recurrence, and time-based movement within a node.

K

Kick

Directed force, push, and momentum transfer into a new path.

Growth path

How a capsule expands

1

Genesis forms. A fully seeded capsule establishes the first protocol ground and publishes the initial relation map.

2

Trust links appear. Proto capsules connect through selective starter sharing and rules-based relationship formation.

3

Network effects compound. Each new capsule adds surface area for exchange while preserving local meaning and autonomy.
