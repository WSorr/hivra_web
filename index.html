Value & Relationship Network

Genesis capsules initiate networks by distributing starters, while Proto capsules grow by forming relationships and participating in value exchange.

Distributed Architecture

Control, trust, and value are distributed across capsules and networks. Coordination emerges from relationships and protocol rules, rather than from central authorities.

Starter System

Five unique starters (Juice, Spark, Seed, Pulse, Kick) act as non-fungible units of value, defining capabilities, permissions, and interaction patterns within the network.
